About

Our efforts focus on both practical solutions, such as adding trash cans to public spaces and hosting community cleanup events, and raising awareness about the harmful effects of litter on our communities and the environment. By advocating the importance of proper waste disposal and empowering the people to take action, we aim to create a culture of accountability and responsibility. We are proud to call El Sauzal de Rodriguez home, where the Valle de Guadalupe meets the sea just north of Ensenada in Baja California. We are taking action and accountability for our neighborhoods and the world beyond. Our mission is to raise awareness and drive change at the grassroots level by promoting responsible waste disposal and clean public spaces. We believe that by taking responsibility for our own actions and spreading awareness, we can create a cleaner and more sustainable future for all.

Mission

Our mission is to drive change at the grassroots level by raising awareness about the harmful effects of litter on our communities and the environment. We aim to empower individuals to take action by educating them on the importance of proper waste disposal and providing access to resources. By working closely with local organizations and hosting community cleanup events, we strive to create a cleaner and more sustainable future, one project at a time.

Together, we can create a cleaner, greener future for all. Be a part of the solution and get involved with Green Armada Las Californias today!
